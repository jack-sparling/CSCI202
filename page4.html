I have a dog, his name is Moose.

He is 3 years old and very rambunctious.

When he was young, we took him to swim.

We threw him a stick and he sprinted to the water.

But when he hit the water... PANIC!

He flailed towards land, refusing to ever return to water.

It took him over a year until finally we said enough.

We got Moose a little pool to get comfortable with the water.

It worked like a charm, and now he can swim like a pro!
